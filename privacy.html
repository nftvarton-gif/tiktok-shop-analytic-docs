Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Tiktok_Shop_Analytic ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

Data Collection

We only collect TikTok Shop data after you authorize our app via OAuth. This includes order data, product data, and finance or performance metrics as provided by the TikTok Shop API. No personal login details are stored by us.

Data Use

Your TikTok Shop data is used only to provide analytics dashboards for your account. We do not share or sell your data to third parties. Access can be revoked at any time from your TikTok developer account.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as long as needed for analytics. If you wish to delete your data, email us at: support@yourdomain.com
